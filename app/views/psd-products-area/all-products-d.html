All products – Search

Search all product records in the Product Safety Database (PSD).

Help with the products search

You can search for one or more products by using the keywords that describe them or use a single PSD reference number to find a specific product record. You can also sort the ordering of the search results.

You can filter the results by hazard type. As an OPSS user you can also include 'retired' product records in your search. (These are records which are no longer active and are now considered less relevant).

Search results appear within the following form and within the role = region.
