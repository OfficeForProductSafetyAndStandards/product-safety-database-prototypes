Create a product record

If a record of a product does not already exist in the Product Safety Database (PSD), you can create a product record for the product. This will also generate a new PSD product record reference number.

The product category will be permanent for this product record
For example, ‘Face mask’ or ‘Washing machine’
Is the product counterfeit?
The counterfeit status will be permanent for this product record. You can create the product record later, or create an alternative product record when required
Does the product have UKCA, UKNI, or CE marking?
Select product marking

Number of units affected?

The number of units affected is case related data (and has now moved to the case page).

For example, Dyson or Sony.
The manufacturer's brand name will be permanent for this product record
Include model name and model number, for example 'PlayStation 5'.
The product name will be permanent for this product record
Was the product placed on the market before 1 January 2021?
Normally 8, 12, or 13 digits

Batch numbers

Batch numbers are case related data (and have now moved to the case page).

For example, serial number, Amazon ID (ASIN) or eBay ID. Use a comma to separate multiple identifiers, and a hyphen to indicate ranges.
The manufacturer’s page for the product, or a link to where it can be bought
Where the product was manufactured

Customs codes

Customs codes are case related data (and have now moved to the case page).

Details about the product you haven’t included above. For example, colour, size, packaging description. Do not include details of damage or incidents
