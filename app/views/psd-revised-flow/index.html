      • Why is the product of concern - edit version
        {Continued} ... with pre-selected where relevant (to reveal their subset forms). Both checkboxes are always disabled (whether pre-selected or not). Enter a reason why the product is unsafe 3. Enter a reason why the product is non-compliant
