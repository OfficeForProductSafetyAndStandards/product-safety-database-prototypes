Create a case

Creating a case starts from a product record page.

Find a product and create the case from there.

How to create a case
  1. In the products area search for your product
  2. Select a product record to view the product record page
  3. Select the 'Create a new case for this product' option
    (in the top-right of a product record page)

When you have finished creating the case, the product will automatically be included.

If a suitable product record does not already exist, create a new product record and then create your case from that product record.
