Hazard type
Lack of protection
Hazard description
In case of a frontal crash, the child car seat (CRS) may not give the protection against head displacement as required.
Affected users
Children 2-3 years
Other users that might be at risk
Not applicable
Step 1

The car seat is occupied by an infant while the vehicle is in use.

Probability of harm: 1 in 1000

Supporting evidence: Sole judgement or estimation







Step 2

The vehicle containing the seat/child is involved in a frontal collision of sufficient magnitude that the protection offered by the seat is necessary.

Probability of harm: 1 in 1000

Supporting evidence: Some limited empirical evidence

Supporting evidence 1.doc










Step 3

The occupant of the seat suffers injury as a result of the collision and the lack of expected protection offered.

Probability of harm: 1 in 2000

Supporting evidence: Some limited empirical evidence

Supporting evidence 2.doc
