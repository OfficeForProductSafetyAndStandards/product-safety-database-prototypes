Determine and evaluate the level of risk presented by a consumer product

The assessment will be undertaken using the recently introduced Product Safety Risk Assessment Methodology (PRISM) which is intended for use by organisations responsible for market surveillance and enforcement.

You can find the PRISM guide, examples of risk assessments and statistical data sets to support your risk assessments on the PRISM guidance page. An explanation of OPSS terminology concerned with risk and risk-related matters can be found on the OPSS risk lexicon page.

Use this service to:

  • Undertake risk triage to determine whether the product needs a full safety risk assessment.
  • Search for products on the Product Safety Database (PSD).
  • Assess and evaluate the risk presenterd by general consumer products.
  • Save and submit assessment results to the PSD.

Before you start

You will need:

  • Product Safety Database (PSD) login credentials - weâ€™ll ask you to sign in or request a new account before starting the full risk assessment.
  • Information about the product, including details of the manufacturer/supplier, number of products sold, and other information accompanying the product.
  • Details of safety concerns associated with the product including number and nature of hazards.

Also, the following information is likely to be useful and relevant in many circumstances:

  • Product test reports.
  • Any relevant research studies, statistical data sets or other evidence to indicate the probability of harm ocurring and the nature of the harm.
  • Any risk assessments that have been undertaken previously for the type of product being assessed or, for closely related products.
