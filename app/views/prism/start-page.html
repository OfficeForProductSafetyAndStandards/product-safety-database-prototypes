Determine and evaluate the level of risk presented by a consumer product

The assessment is part of the recently introduced Product Safety Risk Assessment Methodology (PRISM) and intended for organisations responsible for market surveillance and enforcement.

Although PRISM is primarily aimed at evaluating the risks associated with non-food consumer goods, regulatory bodies in charge of other product categories may also find it beneficial, either in whole or in part.

You can find the PRISM guide, case studies and statistical data sets to support your risk assessments on the guidance page. The explanation of OPSS terminology concerned with risk and risk-related matters can be found on the OPSS risk lexicon page.

Use this service to:

  • Risk triage to determine whether the product needs a full safety risk assessment.
  • Search for products on the Product Safety Database (PSD).
  • Assess and evaluate the risk level of the non-food consumer product.
  • Save and submit assessment results to the PSD.

  • Risk assessments that have been undertaken previously for the product or closely related products.
