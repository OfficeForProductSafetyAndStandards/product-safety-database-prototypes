Is the product likely to pose a serious risk that would justify exemption from a full risk assessment?

For example, products containing prohibited chemicals, non-compliant category III personal protective equipment and other products subject to OPSS safety alerts are likely to present a serious risk of harm and exempt from a full risk assessment.

Help with understanding serious risk products

In some limited circumstances it is appropriate to treat a non-compliant product as likely to present a serious risk, without the need to undertake a full risk assessment.
